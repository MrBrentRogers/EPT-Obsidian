Date:         Mon, 30 Dec 91 15:41:06 EDT
From: Bob Dushay <ROYER%NYSPI.bitnet@CUNYVM.CUNY.EDU>
Subject:      Another information request

The translation of statistics for the EPT creatures list to S&G
standards is very welcome, but what of updated encounter tables,
or at least a list of the types of environments where the
creatures can be found?  It's a safe bet that most of the big
predators that might prey on humans are found in wildernesses
and not on the Sakbe roads, but (for example) would a Zrne
be found in jungles, mountains, or savannahs?  A Mnor?
What creatures haunt the Chakan forests?  This kind of information
would be very helpful.

Subject: Tekumel animals habitats

Here is what I generated for my GURPS conversions. These habitats
correspond to those given in the GURPS Bestiary and Fantasy Bestiary.
I added the differentiation between "Mountains, foothills" and 
"Mountains, peaks". There is also the "Artic" designation that I
left off, because, of course, very few places on Tekumel are artic.
As for encounter tables, I don't use them, so I didn't include any.
