# ‘Harnessing the Tides of Time’ by Juchéssa Betránian of Tsámra
https://www.tekumel.com/gaming_advHTTOT.html

A magical scroll by Jack Bramah

The scroll is in Bednálljan and contains four basic spells with six levels each:

**1.** Travel forwards through time  
**2.** Travel backwards through time  
**3.** Bring objects forward through time  
**4.** Bring objects back from the future  
**5.** Rewind time 1-100 minutes (PT: 3T) T20

#### Levels:

|   |   |   |
|---|---|---|
|1-100 minutes  <br>1-100 hours  <br>1-100 days  <br>1-100 months  <br>1-100 years  <br>1-100 centuries|T7/T9  <br>T8/T10  <br>T9/T11  <br>T10/T13  <br>T12/T15  <br>T14/T17|PT:3CR  <br>PT:10CR  <br>PT:1T  <br>PT:5T  <br>PT:10T  <br>PT:20T|

The two T level numbers for each level represent the effective level for spells 1 and 2 and 3 and 4 respectively. These levels are for random changes to time. To obtain closer control of the span of time (within 1 unit) add 3 to the level.

There is also an extreme danger in using the first two spells. If one comes within 50m of oneself (from a different time period) the caster must save as though against a spell cast by himself or die irrevocably.
