    Originally Posted by Shemek hiTankolel
    General question for the thread. Does anyone know if Phil, or anyone else, made a map of Purdimal? If so, where can I find it? Thanks in advance.

    Shemek.
    Yes. Phil did one of his small (8 1/2 x 11) sketch maps, and Tom Pierik used that to collaborate with Phil on large (18 x 24) maps of the city. Tom originally submitted color maps to AGI, and then black-and-white as we could not do color maps back then. I believe - I'd have to check my inventory notes on his collection - that the color maps wound up with Phil, and thus with the TF. I have the black-and-whites, in several different hard-copy versions, and I'll have to check our collections index to see if we have them in the digital files.
