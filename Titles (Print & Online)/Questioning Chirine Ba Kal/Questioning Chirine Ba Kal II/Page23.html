    Originally Posted by Neshm hiKumala
    Chirine, I'm reading the P Chi article found in one of the Seal of the Imperium journals. It mentions a detail which I never thought about before: there's probably P Chi technology going back to Humanspace on Tkumel. The P Chi themselves don't seem to be interested in it, at all. But this suggests, of course, that there is alien tech from other non-human species scattered around too; tech from the Shn, or the Hlutrg, etc.

    In your adventures, did you ever come across technology that was specifically alien or non-human?
