    Howard,

    Unfortunately the Tekumel forums, to put it kindly, are as moribund as Lord Sarku's minions. Years between new threads and a few postings here and there is not what I would call a lively debate. There simply is no real exchange of ideas which makes it useless to me. I truly salute your effort to get that venue re-energised, but Tekumel.com is a shadow of what it used to be. Too bad really.

    I agree with Hrugga's assessment regarding this thread, and it completely mirrors my feelings towards it, and quite frankly this is the only reason that I am even active on this forum. Perhaps we could get something similar going on one of the other Tekumel Groups, but I have a bad feeling that the politics would quickly start to manifest and bugger things up. If, as you say, the bullshit could be kept out I would be totally onboard with a more Tekumel dedicated milieu.
